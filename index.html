Lancaster Dental – Cosmetic and Family Dentistry

Lancaster Dental, a Kitchener Dentist Office has been serving communities since 1978. We are proud to say that our services have always left patients happy after every procedure. We protect people’s beautiful smiles through our dental cleaning and prevention services, which include dental exams and cleaning, X-rays, fluoride treatment, and more. For even better enhancements, we offer custom-made Porcelain Veneers that will absolutely intensify the effect of perfect teeth. With the addition of our advanced Teeth Whitening procedures, we guarantee confidence and pride as every tooth is shown off. Invisalign or Teeth Alignment – If there is a need for teeth alignment, our Invisalign procedure is up to the job. We use invisible aligners that are easy to remove and involve no metal wires to encourage dental alignment. In a short time, you will get perfectly straight, even teeth without the pain of wearing braces or brackets. Whether your dental needs are cosmetic dentistry treatment, wisdom tooth removal, or a full mouth restorative, we promise to provide you with exceptional care as we enhance the natural beauty of your smile. Below are just some of the many procedures and services we regularly provide to our patients – with a gentle touch, and stunning results. Your smile is our first priority, and we’ll give you something to smile about.
